Missing PyCamp? Welcome, PyCon!

Wednesday, May 19, 2010 by Volodymyr Hotsyk

At the beginning of the 2010, the conference PyCamp gathered about 220 participants from the Ukraine, Russia and Belarus into the magical Python world. We decided to continue this great happening and today we are starting the preparation to the biggest python conference in Kyiv. This Fall.

We are open for proposals and your feedback. Let's make it the most impressive Python event together!
