Mongo - one year later.

Monday, July 12, 2010 by Volodymyr Hotsyk

Marcin Szajek (Programa.pl) and Maciej Dziardziel (Adwertajzing) will speak about their experience of using MongoDB in production.

As creators of social networking sites totutam.pl, similaria.pl and ad network adwerti.pl, we want to talk about our experience with Mongodb, one year after we started using it. We will tell about the reasons that led us into NoSQL database, how Mongo have influenced the tools we are using, the way we design our applications and how it meets our expectations. We will also present some of tools that we have built to make working with Mongo easier.

Missing PyCamp? Welcome, PyCon!

Wednesday, May 19, 2010 by Volodymyr Hotsyk

At the beginning of the 2010, the conference PyCamp gathered about 220 participants from Ukraine, Russia and Belarus into the magical Python world. We decided to continue this great happening and today we are starting the preparation to the biggest python conference in Kyiv. This Fall.

We are open for proposals and your feedback. Let's make it the most impressive Python event together!
